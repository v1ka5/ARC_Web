International Mobile Top Up

Add funds to prepaid phones of friends and family around the world. It's simple, safe and instant.
  • Benefits
  • How It Works
  • Redeem a Carrier Top Up Card

To Get Started,
Enter an International Mobile Phone Number, Beginning with Country Code

Benefits

Easy

Give the gift of talk -- just select a top up amount and the airtime minutes will be on their way.

Worldwide

Recharge mobile phones in over 200 destinations from our website or our award-winning mobile app.

Safe & Instant

Be confident that the phone abroad will be recharged safely and instantly with the amount that you requested.

How It Works

Enter the Phone Number

Enter the international mobile number you’d like to recharge.

Confirm the Mobile Carrier

Confirm your loved one's phone carrier is correct or update to the appropriate carrier.

Choose an Amount

Choose the amount that you’d like to send to this phone number.

Redeem a Carrier Top Up Card

The total value of the prepaid card will be added to this top up.

Your PIN is found on the back of your card.
